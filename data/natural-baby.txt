Company Overview:
Natural Baby is a modern baby-care brand dedicated to providing safe, gentle, and eco-conscious products for newborns and toddlers. Founded in 2018, Natural Baby focuses on clean ingredients, hypoallergenic materials, and clinically tested safety standards. Our mission is to create products that simplify parenting while ensuring maximum comfort and protection for babies. All Natural Baby products are dermatologically tested, cruelty-free, and made using responsibly sourced materials.

About Natural Baby:
Natural Baby was started by a group of young parents who struggled to find products that were both safe and effective. Our brand is built on three core values: safety, transparency, and comfort. We believe that every parent deserves peace of mind, and every baby deserves products that nurture their growth without exposing them to harmful chemicals. Today, Natural Baby serves families in more than 20 countries and continues to expand with innovative solutions in feeding, sleep, skincare, travel, and play.
